NOUNS

A noun represents a person, place, animal, thing or an idea.


Kinds of noun

Nouns are classified as follows:

  • Phoper:Is is the name of a particular object, whether person, place, animal, idea or thing.
  • Common:Is is the name possesesed by things in general.
  • Collective:It is the name representing a group of things.
  • Abstract: It is the name representing qualities, emotions, conditions or actions.

PRONOUNS

A pronoun is a word used in place of a noun. A pronoun is generally used to avoid repetition of a noun.


Kinds of pronouns

Pronouns are classified as follows:

  • Personal pronouns:Is refers to a specific noun and used as a substitute of the noun.
  • Interrogative pronouns:Is stands as the answer to the question about a person, place or a thing.
  • Indefinite pronouns: It's used to refer to a person, thing or a place that are unknown.
  • Relative pronouns: It relates to a noun, pronoun, word or expression precedding it in the sentence, and at the same time connects two statements.
  • Demonstrative pronouns: A nouns or pronoun is specified and identified by it
  • Reflexive pronouns: It usually follows a noun or a pronoun and used to emphasize the subject.
